About me

I am now an Assitant Professor at UIBE in Beijing, previously held a visiting researcher position in the computational statistics group at TU Vienna, Austria and obtained my Ph.D. degree of statistics in 2019 from the Department of Statistics, Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, under the supervision of Dr. David E. Tyler. Before that, I studied physics with a B.Sc. degree from Southeast University, Nanjing, China.

Research

My research interests include

  • Multivariate statistics

  • Spatial statistics

  • Robust statistics

  • Time series analysis

  • High dimensional statistics
