About me

I am now a Lecturer at Beijing Normal University, previously employed as an assistant professor at University of International Business and Economics and as a research associate in the computational statistics group at TU Vienna, Austria, and obtained her Ph.D. degree of statistics in 2019 from the Department of Statistics, Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, under the supervision of Prof. David E. Tyler. Before that, I studied physics with a B.Sc. degree from Southeast University, Nanjing, China.
