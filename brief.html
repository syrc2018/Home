About me

Mengxi Yi is now an Assitant Professor at UIBE in Beijing, previously held a visiting researcher position in the computational statistics group at TU Vienna, Austria and obtained her Ph.D. degree of statistics in 2019 from the Department of Statistics, Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey, under the supervision of Prof. David E. Tyler. Before that, she studied physics with a B.Sc. degree from Southeast University, Nanjing, China.
